**part_triangles.out**

60 triangles defined by three (x,y,z) corner locations, making up the two divertors of 30 triangles each.

**part_triangle_mids.out**

Mid-points of triangles

**allparts.out**

Data from parts-list reformatted

**surface_line.out**

Fieldline followed from (rstart, zstart, phistart) without diffusion to make a LCFS approximation.

**launch_pts.out**

Launch (starting) location points of the # fieldlines that are traced

**int_pts.out**

Points of intersection between each fieldline and vessel / divertor surfaces

**hitline.out**

Locations of the last section of the fieldline at and before strikepoint (only if intersected with divertor)

**hitcount.out**

Number of points that hit a divertor / vessel surface vs not hitting anything
